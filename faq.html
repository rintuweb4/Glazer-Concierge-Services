What is your Privacy Policy

All personal and credit card information collected by Glazer Concierge will remain confidential. We do not share or sell any information about our clients. We are discreet and confidential.

Each employee also sign iron-clad confidentiality agreement to protect the privacy of every client. We believe every foundation of any successful relationship is Trust. This is why we take security and discretion so seriously and ensure that each employee know how to properly store and handle their client’s sensitive data.

What does Home Watch mean?

It is a service we provides offering you peace of mind while you are away from your most valuable investment , your home. We believe your investment should not be a source of worry when you are away from it.

We visit your home, condo or apartment at agreed upon times and conduct a thorough inspection of every property. We pick up your email, water your plant, we are your eyes and ears when you cannot be. We are fully bonded and insured.
